Habitat

Habitat: Lives on rocky or sandy bottoms covered in algae. From the sublittoral zone up to 150 meters, sometimes even 600 meters.

Carapace Size

Carapace size can reach 25 cm in length and 18 cm in width.

Diet

Feeds on algae, sea urchins, starfish, crustaceans, polychaetes, mollusks, and fish.

Geographical Distribution

Mediterranean Sea: from Spain to northern Slovenia and eastern Turkey. Atlantic Ocean: from Ireland to Guinea.

Spider crabs undergo periodic molting to facilitate growth. After the terminal molt, growth ceases.
